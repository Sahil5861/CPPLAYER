News 24 is a popular 24-hour Hindi news television channel in India, offering continuous news coverage on a variety of topics including politics, current events, sports, entertainment, and international news. Launched in 2007, it is part of the BAG Network and is known for its in-depth analysis, breaking news updates, and special programming. The channel aims to keep viewers informed with accurate and timely reporting, making it a trusted source of news for millions of Hindi-speaking audiences. News 24 also engages viewers through its digital platforms, extending its reach beyond traditional television.
